About Me

I am Horse Man. I am the child of two centaurs and through unlucky genetics, I was born 100% horse bodied with a human mind. While this caused incessant bullying and other challenges in my younger years, these unique traits of mine gave me a keen sense of otherness that drove me to empathize with others and encourage equity and kindess in all sorts of circles.
Due to my constant interest in politics and the experiences of youth, I am a driven educator at Centaurus High School and hope to continue shaping young minds.
